Trusted Adults


A trusted adult is someone you have a good relationship with and has your best interests in mind that you feel comfortable expressing feelings or concerns.

School Guidance Counselors

A school guidance counselor is a professional who works in schools to provide academic, career, college, and social-emotional competencies to all students through a school counseling program.

Teachers or Mentors

Teachers and mentors help relay knowledge to students. They often act as an advisor to facilitate students' personal development.


School Psychologist

School Psychologists have an advanced degree in psychology. They are trained to make diagnoses, provide individual and group therapy, and work with school staff to maximize efficiency in the school setting.

Clinical Psychologist

Psychologists have at least a doctoral degree in psychology. They are trained to make diagnoses and provide individual and group therapy.
